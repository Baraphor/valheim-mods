Potted Plants - 1.0.0
This mod adjusts the building costs of stone building pieces to be relative to their size. On average building, stone structures will now be cheaper than before, but better balanced.

:: REQUIREMENTS
• Valheim
• BepInEx Pack Valheim
• Jotunn Lib

:: FEATURES
• Yellow Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Wood
• Blue Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Blueberry, 1 Wood
• Orange Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Carrot, 1 Wood
• Red Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Raspberry, 1 Wood
• Purple Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Turnip, 1 Wood
• White Flower Pot - 1 Dandelion, 1 Bone Fragment, 1 Wood
• Thistle Flower Pot - 1 Thistle, 1 Wood, Yes it glows
